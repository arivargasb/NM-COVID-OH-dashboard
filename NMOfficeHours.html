NM Infectious Disease Office Hours

Trends in the topics covered:

4/15/2020 - 5/10/2021


This website shows the trends of a variety of COVID-19 terms that have been discussed over the course of the NM Infectious Disease Office Hours program's sessions. The objective is to contribute to characterizing the clinical course of the disease by looking at the patterns in the contents covered, which reflect new insights that emerge both from ongoing research and from practitioners' experiences treating COVID-19 patients, as well as the information needs from program's participants.
